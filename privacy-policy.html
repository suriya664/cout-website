Privacy Policy

Updated October 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Constructa is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our website, client portals, and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, and company details when you submit forms or request services. Usage data—including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited—is collected to improve user experience and security.

3. How We Use Information

Information supports project collaboration, service delivery, marketing communications, and compliance obligations. We only process data for legitimate business purposes and in accordance with applicable laws.

4. Data Sharing

Constructa may share information with trusted partners and subcontractors under confidentiality agreements. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards including encryption, access controls, and continuous monitoring to protect information against unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact privacy@constructa.com to exercise your rights, or for questions about this policy.

7. Updates

We review and update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via our website or direct notification.
